Welcome to SPFadvisor

Our application offers an analysis tool that helps users pick the best sunscreen for their skin. Utilizing research data and even your input, a tailored set of specific ingredients and products will be offered to you. Experienced sunscreen users can provide their experiences with previous sunscreens to get very precise suggestions. Even if this will be your first sunscreen, we will offer the best products to help you achieve your skin goals. This versatile tool not only offers suggestions but also warns you of harmful ingredients. Categories such as “Environmentally safe”, “Harmful to skin”, and “comedogenic” will help a user understand their products.

Sunscreen is one of the most important steps of your skincare routine. It is also the product that is said to fight the most against anti-aging. Not only that, sunscreen is vital in preventing serious health issues such as skin cancer. After seeing the many consequences that could come with not wearing sunscreen, we decided to make all of these features free. We feel that the implications that could occur from not using sunscreen are too much to be locked behind a paywall. Therefore, we hope that you enjoy our application.
